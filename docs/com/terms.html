Orders, Payments, and Refunds

  • Any prices listed publicly are estimates and I may adjust them depending on the particular work requested.
  • I typically ask for half the price to be paid in advance, and the other half on completion.
  • If you cancel your order after I've started working on it, you are not entitled to a refund of your advance payment.
  • I may deny any commission request for any reason.
  • I may cancel and refund a commission in progress at any point.

Progress Guarantee

  • If I make no meaningful progress on your unfinished commission for 8 weeks or more, and I am not awaiting a response from you, you may consider me to have cancelled it, and are thus entitled to a full refund.
