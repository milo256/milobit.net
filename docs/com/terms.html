** By commissioning my art, you agree to these terms. Any modifications must be communicated in advance **

Orders, Payments, and Refunds

  • I currently accept payment through paypal only
  • The prices listed on the commission sheet are estimates and I may adjust them depending on the particular work requested.
  • Half of the payment must be provided up-front, with the remaining amount paid upon completion.
  • If you cancel your order after I have started working, I am not obligated to refund your advance payment.
  • Expect work-in-progress images to have a reduced resolution and/or watermark.
  • I may deny any commission request for any reason.
  • I may cancel and refund an order at any point.

Rights and Licensing
